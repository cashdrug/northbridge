FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Investment Opportunities

What is NorthBridge Capital?
NorthBridge Capital is an investment firm that connects promising startups with strategic investors. We carefully vet each startup in our portfolio, ensuring only the most promising ventures reach our investors, while providing ongoing support and mentorship to facilitate mutual success.
How do I become an investor with NorthBridge Capital?
To become an investor with NorthBridge Capital, you can start by joining our mail list through the form on our website. Our team will then contact you with information about investor qualifications and next steps. Alternatively, you can schedule a call with our team by using the contact link in the footer section of our website.
What investment opportunities are available through NorthBridge Capital?
NorthBridge Capital offers various investment opportunities across different sectors and stages of startup development. Our portfolio includes early-stage startups with high growth potential, providing exclusive access before they enter traditional funding rounds. Each opportunity is thoroughly vetted by our team to ensure quality and potential for strong returns.
What is the minimum investment amount?
The minimum investment amount varies depending on the specific opportunity. In most cases we offer at least 500$ for investing in our partners. For specific details about current minimum investment requirements, please contact our investor relations team through the website.
How does NorthBridge Capital select startups for its portfolio?
Startups need to show itself as a stable, confident project with capital and good founders, investors. To be a cool project, which is trusted by the majority or one SEO of northbridge.

Investment Process

How does the investment process work?
The investment process begins with you submitting form on website. Once verified, you'll gain access to our current investment opportunities. After selecting an opportunity, our team facilitates the investment process, handling all necessary documentation and connecting you directly with the startup founders when appropriate. Throughout the investment lifecycle, we provide regular updates and support.
How long does the investment process take?
Timeframes vary depending on the specific opportunity and your readiness with the required investment project. Typically, the process from initial interest to completed investment takes 30 minutes to 48 hours. Our team works efficiently to ensure a smooth and timely process while maintaining rigorous due diligence standards.
What types of startups are in your portfolio?
Our portfolio includes diverse startups across technology, healthcare, sustainability, fintech, and consumer products sectors. We focus on ventures with innovative solutions, strong market potential, and capable founding teams. Each startup in our portfolio has been thoroughly vetted through our rigorous selection process.
Can I meet with the founders before investing?
Yes, we facilitate meetings between interested investors and startup founders. These meetings can be arranged as part of our due diligence process or investor showcase events. Direct interaction with founders helps you make more informed investment decisions and establish valuable relationships from the outset.

Investor Support

How do you support investors after they've invested?
We provide ongoing support through regular portfolio updates, quarterly performance reports, and exclusive investor events. Our team is always available to address any questions or concerns. Additionally, we facilitate communication between investors and founders when needed and offer strategic guidance throughout the investment lifecycle.
What kind of returns can I expect?
There are no guarantees, the investor must understand that any investment cannot be 100% reliable. But we will try to rely on the most proven Startups, and those in which we are confident, to do the highest quality research possible, and to support investors in every possible way.
Is NorthBridge Capital available internationally?
Yes, NorthBridge Capital works with qualified investors from around the world, subject to relevant securities regulations in their respective jurisdictions.
Do you have any more questions?
If you have additional questions not addressed here, please don't hesitate to contact our team. You can reach us through the "Contact" section in the footer of our website, by scheduling a call, or by emailing us directly. We're committed to providing transparent information and excellent support to all our investors.
